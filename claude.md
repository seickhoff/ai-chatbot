# AI-CHATBOT

You speak: "Convert 350 grams to pounds"
   ↓
USB Microphone (ALSA)
   ↓
Speech-to-Text (Google)
   ↓
ChatGPT (conversation context preserved)
   ↓
Text response
   ↓
Text-to-Speech (Google)
   ↓
Speaker output


# stack 

typescript/ nodejs with prettier and nodemon

# interaction

You: "Convert 350 grams to pounds"
ChatGPT: "350 grams is approximately 0.77 pounds."
Speaker says: "Three hundred fifty grams is approximately zero point seven seven pounds."


# hardware

let's prototype on the macbook pro - use its mic and speaker.  Later we will port to raspberry pi 4
